## Day 1 - This project.

This is a side project I've decided to recently start for when I'm a little bored. I don't like how people are getting less and less creative, so I want to create a portfolio that showcases how people can create what *they* want to make.

People are too caught up with getting into university, getting a job, they're not appreciating building stuff or doing stuff that *they* want to do. This website will be a cool experiment where it's just me adding what *I* want to add to my website, hopefully creating a cool illusion.

## Day 2 - Thought

Thought is kind of underestimated in the modern world. People think too quickly and apprehensively instead of just thinking on something before doing. I really like thinking hard and researching on my projects before making them and this project is no different.
